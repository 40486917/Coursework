Side Street Burgers takeaway in Bonnyrigg is a great option for anyone looking for delicious burgers on-the-go. The restaurant is small and unassuming, but the quality of the food is outstanding.

The menu at Side Street Burgers takeaway is focused on high-quality, homemade burgers, featuring a range of creative and delicious options. The standout dishes include the classic cheeseburger, the crispy chicken burger, and the vegetarian burger made with a tasty veggie patty. All burgers are cooked to order and come with a range of delicious toppings, including bacon, cheese, and caramelized onions.

The sides at Side Street Burgers takeaway are equally delicious, with highlights including the crispy onion rings and the loaded fries, which come with a variety of toppings like bacon and cheese sauce. The milkshakes are also a must-try, made with high-quality ice cream and a range of flavors to choose from.

The service at Side Street Burgers takeaway is efficient and friendly, with the staff working hard to ensure that your food is hot and ready for pickup on time. The prices are also very reasonable, making it a great option for anyone looking for delicious burgers at an affordable price.

Overall, Side Street Burgers takeaway in Bonnyrigg is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for high-quality burgers and sides on-the-go. Whether you're in the mood for a quick and easy meal or a feast to share with friends, Side Street Burgers has got you covered. Highly recommended.
