Tattu in Edinburgh is an upscale Chinese restaurant that offers a unique and memorable dining experience. The restaurant is stunning, with a beautiful interior design that features cherry blossom trees and intricate wall art, creating a sophisticated and romantic atmosphere.

The menu at Tattu is modern and creative, with a fusion of traditional Chinese flavors and contemporary techniques. The dishes are all beautifully presented, with a focus on bold flavors and high-quality ingredients.

The standout dishes at Tattu include the black cod with miso, the crispy duck salad, and the wagyu beef with truffle soy. The dim sum is also excellent, with a range of innovative and delicious options to choose from.

The service at Tattu is exceptional, with the staff being knowledgeable, friendly, and attentive, making you feel welcome and comfortable throughout your meal. The restaurant also offers an extensive drinks menu, including a variety of cocktails and a carefully curated wine list.

While the prices at Tattu are on the higher side, the quality of the food, service, and atmosphere more than justify the cost. Overall, Tattu in Edinburgh is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for a unique and upscale dining experience with delicious and creative Chinese cuisine. Highly recommended.
