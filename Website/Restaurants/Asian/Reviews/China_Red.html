China Red on Grindlay Street in Edinburgh is a fantastic restaurant that offers authentic Chinese cuisine in a modern and stylish setting. The restaurant is spacious and elegant, with contemporary decor and a welcoming atmosphere.

The menu at China Red is extensive and features a wide range of Chinese dishes, including Sichuan, Cantonese, and Beijing-style cuisine. The dishes are all expertly prepared, with fresh and high-quality ingredients and beautifully balanced flavors.

The standout dishes at China Red include the hot and sour soup, the Kung Pao chicken, and the crispy duck pancakes. The dim sum is also excellent, with a variety of dumplings and steamed buns to choose from.

The service at China Red is top-notch, with the attentive and friendly staff going out of their way to make sure you have an enjoyable dining experience. The restaurant also offers an excellent selection of wines and cocktails to accompany your meal.

While the prices at China Red are a bit higher than other Chinese restaurants, the quality of the food and the service more than justifies the cost. Overall, China Red on Grindlay Street in Edinburgh is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for authentic and delicious Chinese cuisine in a stylish and welcoming setting. Highly recommended.
