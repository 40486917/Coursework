Franco Manca is a popular pizza chain that has recently opened a branch in Edinburgh, and it's safe to say that it has been well worth the wait. The restaurant has a relaxed and casual atmosphere with rustic decor and an open kitchen, giving diners a front-row seat to the pizza-making process.

The menu at Franco Manca is relatively simple, with a selection of sourdough pizzas and a few sides. However, the pizzas themselves are anything but ordinary. The sourdough base is light and crispy, with just the right amount of chewiness, and the toppings are fresh, high-quality, and expertly balanced. The standout pizzas include the classic Margherita, the spicy Nduja, and the indulgent Wild Mushroom.

The restaurant also offers an excellent selection of drinks, including wine, beer, and cocktails, to accompany your meal. The staff is knowledgeable about the menu and happy to make recommendations, and the service is efficient and friendly.

While the prices at Franco Manca are a bit higher than other pizza places, the quality of the food more than justifies the cost. Overall, Franco Manca in Edinburgh is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for delicious and authentic sourdough pizza in a relaxed and casual setting. Highly recommended.
