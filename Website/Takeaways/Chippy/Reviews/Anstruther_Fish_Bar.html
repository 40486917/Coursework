The Anstruther Fish Bar, located in the picturesque fishing village of Anstruther in Scotland, is an institution when it comes to fish and chips. The restaurant has been serving up delicious seafood for over 50 years and has won numerous awards for its excellent food and service.

The menu at the Anstruther Fish Bar is simple and focused on high-quality, fresh seafood. The standout dish, of course, is the classic fish and chips, made with locally sourced haddock and perfectly cooked to order. The batter is light and crispy, and the fish is always fresh and flaky.

In addition to fish and chips, the Anstruther Fish Bar also serves other seafood dishes, such as smoked salmon and Cullen skink soup. The sides, including mushy peas and tartar sauce, are all made in-house and add the perfect accompaniment to the main course.

The service at the Anstruther Fish Bar is efficient and friendly, with the staff always happy to help with any questions or recommendations. The restaurant is popular and can get busy, but the staff work hard to ensure that everyone is served quickly and with a smile.

Overall, the Anstruther Fish Bar is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for delicious seafood and a taste of Scottish coastal life. The restaurant is well worth the journey and is a testament to the quality and pride that goes into making the best fish and chips. Highly recommended.
