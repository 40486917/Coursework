Lucky House Chinese Takeaway in Dundee is a hidden gem that offers exceptional Chinese cuisine for takeaway. The restaurant itself is small and unassuming, but the quality of the food is outstanding.

The menu at Lucky House Chinese Takeaway is extensive, featuring a wide range of Chinese dishes, including classic favorites like sweet and sour chicken and fried rice, as well as more adventurous options like salt and chilli squid and ma po tofu. All dishes are freshly prepared to order, with high-quality ingredients and generous portions.

The standout dishes at Lucky House Chinese Takeaway include the crispy duck pancakes, the prawn toast, and the black bean beef. The noodles and fried rice dishes are also excellent, with perfectly cooked grains and flavorful sauces.

The service at Lucky House Chinese Takeaway is friendly and efficient, with the staff being knowledgeable about the menu and happy to make recommendations. The prices are also very reasonable, making it a great option for anyone looking for delicious Chinese food at an affordable price.

Overall, Lucky House Chinese Takeaway in Dundee is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for high-quality Chinese cuisine for takeaway. Whether you're in the mood for a quick and easy meal or a feast to share with friends, Lucky House Chinese Takeaway has got you covered. Highly recommended.
