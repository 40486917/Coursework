Gigi's Italian restaurant in Bonnyrigg is a hidden gem that offers a truly authentic Italian dining experience. The restaurant is cozy and inviting, with warm lighting and charming decor that immediately transports you to Italy.

The menu at Gigi's is extensive and features all the classic Italian dishes you would expect, as well as some unique and creative options. The pasta dishes are particularly noteworthy, with each dish cooked to perfection and bursting with flavor. The homemade pizzas are also a must-try, with perfectly crispy crusts and delicious toppings.

The restaurant also offers an excellent selection of wines and cocktails to accompany your meal, and the knowledgeable staff is always happy to make recommendations.

The service at Gigi's is impeccable, with the friendly and attentive staff going out of their way to make sure you have an enjoyable dining experience. The restaurant is also very reasonably priced, making it a great value for money option.

Overall, Gigi's Italian restaurant in Bonnyrigg is a fantastic spot for anyone looking for authentic Italian cuisine in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're in the mood for pasta, pizza, or anything in between, Gigi's has got you covered. Highly recommended.
